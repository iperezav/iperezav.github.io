Ivan Perez Avellaneda

I am a Ph.D. student in Electrical Engineering at the University of Vermont in the U.S. I work in the group of Dr. Luis Duffaut Espinosa. I hold a Master's degree in Economics and a Bachelor's and License degree in Mathematics by the Pontifica Universidad Católica del Perú.

My current research merges Inifinite Dimension Real Analysis, Nonlinear Control Theory, Non-commutative Algebra and Optimization. Specifically, I work on Reachability Analysis of non-linear systems which involves extending optimization methods to Chen-Fliess series. Prior to this I have worked on Algebraic Geometry in Mathematics and Debt-Control in Economics.
